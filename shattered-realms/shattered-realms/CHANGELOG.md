# Changelog

All notable changes to The Shattered Realms module will be documented in this file.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-02-08

### Added
- Initial release of The Shattered Realms campaign module
- Complete world setting with lore and background
- 10+ unique NPCs and creatures including:
  - Valdris the Shattered (main antagonist)
  - Grommak Shadowfang (orc chieftain)
  - Rift Trolls, Rift Goblins, Rift Demons
  - Twilight Druids and corrupted creatures
- 10+ magic items with rift-themed abilities:
  - Riftblade (very rare longsword)
  - Amulet of Planar Anchoring
  - Staff of Reality Weaving
  - And more...
- 5 comprehensive rollable tables:
  - Random Rift Encounters
  - Rift Treasure
  - Wilderness Encounters - Ironpeak
  - Twilight Forest Encounters
  - Rift City Random Events
- 5+ detailed journal entries:
  - Campaign Guide
  - Locations Guide
  - Quest: The Broken Tower
  - Quest: Shadows of Ironpeak
  - Bestiary - Rift Creatures
- 4 scene templates:
  - Rift City - Central Plaza
  - The Broken Tower - Level 1
  - Shadowfang Hold - Great Hall
  - Twilight Forest - Heartwood Grove
- Comprehensive GM Guide with running tips
- Installation and setup documentation
- Compatible with D&D 5e system version 2.0.0+
- Compatible with Foundry VTT version 11+

### Features
- Weeks of playable content for levels 1-10+
- Flexible campaign structure (linear or sandbox)
- Rich faction system with competing interests
- Unique rift magic system
- Multiple quest lines and adventure hooks
- Detailed NPC personalities and motivations
- Scalable encounters and challenges
