# The Shattered Realms - Complete D&D 5e Campaign

## Overview

Welcome to **The Shattered Realms**, a comprehensive campaign module for Foundry Virtual Tabletop using the D&D 5th Edition system. This module provides weeks of adventure content in a rich fantasy world where ancient magic has fractured reality, creating rifts between planes and unleashing chaos across the land.

## Features

- **10+ Detailed Scenes** - Fully mapped locations including towns, dungeons, wilderness areas, and magical anomalies
- **50+ Actors** - Pre-configured NPCs, monsters, and creatures including orcs, trolls, goblins, dragons, demons, and more
- **100+ Items** - Weapons, armor, magical artifacts, and treasure
- **Extensive Lore** - Journal entries detailing the world's history, factions, and locations
- **Combat Encounters** - Pre-balanced encounters for parties levels 1-10
- **Rollable Tables** - Random encounters, treasure generation, and world events
- **Complete Adventure Path** - Interconnected quests and storylines for extended gameplay

## Installation

### Method 1: Module Browser
1. Open Foundry VTT and navigate to the **Add-on Modules** tab
2. Click **Install Module**
3. Search for "Shattered Realms"
4. Click **Install**

### Method 2: Manifest URL
1. Open Foundry VTT and navigate to the **Add-on Modules** tab
2. Click **Install Module**
3. Paste this manifest URL: `https://github.com/yourusername/shattered-realms/releases/latest/download/module.json`
4. Click **Install**

### Method 3: Manual Installation
1. Download the latest release ZIP file
2. Extract to your Foundry VTT `Data/modules` directory
3. Restart Foundry VTT

## Usage

1. **Create a new world** using the D&D 5e system (or use an existing one)
2. **Enable the module** in your world's module settings
3. **Import the Adventure** from the compendium: `The Shattered Realms - Main Adventure`
4. Alternatively, import individual elements from the various compendium packs

## The World

### The Cataclysm

A thousand years ago, a war between archmages nearly destroyed the world. Their final battle tore reality itself, creating the **Shattered Rifts** - permanent tears in the fabric of space that leak wild magic and connect to other planes. The land was broken into isolated regions, and civilization collapsed.

### The Present Day

The world is slowly recovering. New kingdoms have risen, but the rifts remain, spawning monsters and warping the landscape. Adventurers are needed to explore ancient ruins, close dangerous rifts, and protect the fragile peace between the races.

### Major Regions

- **The Fractured Kingdoms** - Human territories struggling to unite
- **Ironpeak Mountains** - Dwarven holds and orc strongholds in conflict
- **The Twilight Forest** - Elven lands corrupted by rift magic
- **The Ashen Wastes** - Desolate lands twisted by planar energy
- **Rift City** - A multicultural hub built around a stabilized rift

## Campaign Structure

The module supports multiple play styles:

### Sandbox Exploration
Use the detailed locations and random encounter tables to create your own adventures in the Shattered Realms.

### Main Quest Line
Follow the included adventure path:
1. **The Broken Tower** (Levels 1-3) - Investigate strange happenings at an abandoned wizard's tower
2. **Shadows of Ironpeak** (Levels 3-5) - Prevent war between dwarves and orcs
3. **The Twilight Curse** (Levels 5-7) - Cleanse corruption from the ancient forest
4. **Rift Runners** (Levels 7-9) - Chase smugglers through planar rifts
5. **The Final Convergence** (Levels 9-10+) - Stop a plot to tear open the rifts permanently
